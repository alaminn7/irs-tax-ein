Services Provided

GovFilingsOnline is a document prep and filing service for certain government documents including federal filings, state filings, and other services. GovFilingsOnline is a document prep and filing service. We are not a government agency, and you can obtain government filings through the issuing government office at a reduced fee or for free by filing on your own. By hiring GovFilingsOnline, you agree to understanding we are a third party filing service, and charge a fee for our service to perform filings.

Vital Record (birth/death) Request Services:

Our birth certificate or vital record filing service generally has two parts:

  1. Gathering and preparing the proper paperwork/application and materials for your specific birth certificate request. This will vary depending on the state/county of birth and type of request, and usually consists of preparing your state birth certificate application(s) or authorization form(s).
  2. Creating customized next steps for the client to complete any additional state requirements to obtain the birth certificate. This may vary by location of birth and the client's relationship to the subject of the record. ​

EIN or Business Filing Services:

Our Federal EIN Filing and Setup service is one where GovFilingsOnline will file for and set up a federal employer identification number (EIN) through the IRS for a client. We will send a computer screenshot of the completed EIN, and the IRS will mail an official hard copy of the EIN.

Passport Renewal Services:

Our passport renewal filing service consists of a few parts:

  1. Gathering and preparing the proper paperwork/application and materials for your specific passport renewal request. This will vary depending on the requestors current passport status and other factors.
  2. Preparing customized next steps to assist in gathering/submitting items that are required by the US Department of State for the passport renewal request.
  3. Preparing a prepaid/pre-addressed USPS Priority Mail label and envelope for the client to submit the required items to the US Department of State/National Passport Processing Center
  4. Delivering all of these items by mail to the client.

About Us

GovFilingsOnline is a private document filing and preparation service specializing in business document filings through state offices and through the IRS. We are not endorsed or affiliated with any government agency, and often times an individual may file directly through the appropriate government office for free or for a reduced fee. Clients use our service to avoid the headache, hassle, and research that accompanies trying to navigate the state and federal filing systems. Don't waste time in line at the government office, don't waste time researching government websites! Let our business filing experts handle the messy work for you!
